Calico Critters Biography Information

Mother Hilda is a teacher in the Forest Nursery. She is also a fantastic play writer adn dance instructor . Critters come from all over Calico Village to see her amazing plays!

Father Harry drives all the children to the nursery in the double decker school bus. He is a very safe driver nad enjoys making the children laugh with his funny jokes.

Daughter Jane would like to be a writer when she grows up and loves creating stories. All her friends especially like her fairytale stories about princes and princesses!
