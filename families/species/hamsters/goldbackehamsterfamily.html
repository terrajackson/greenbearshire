Sylvanian Families Goldbacke Hamster Family

UK Biography Information

Mother Emma Goldbacke works at Forest Nursery as the kindergarten teacher. Every day the young Sylvanian toddlers come to Forest Nursery to make friends, play, sing, dance and learn their ABCs and Emma is the perfect teacher for them.

Father Nick Goldbacke also works at Forest Nursery as the Nursery Double Decker Bus driver! He drives round the Sylvanian village every morning picking up all the Sylvanian toddlers from their houses and drops them off for nursery where they are looked after by his wife Emma.

Sister Nora Goldbacke is an aspiring writer! She recently wrote the script for the production of 'Prince Charming' at Country Tree School and all the girls in Sylvania are exciting about auditioning for the role of the princess!

Brother Frank Goldbacke dreams of becoming a professional athlete. Every lunch time you will find him out on the running track behind Country Tree School trying to improve on his best times for the 100m sprint and the 200m hurdles.
