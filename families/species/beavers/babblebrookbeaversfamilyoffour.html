US Biography Information

Wade Babblebrook is the most popular dentist in Cloverleaf Corners. Children love how painless dental check-ups are. His favoite hobby is to lecture everyone who crosses his path about the importance of brushing his or her teeth.

Sandy Babblebrook loves to sit along the riverbank and paint landscapes of the beautiful countryside. Many of her paintings hang in Father Babblebrook's Dental Office. Sandy volunteers as a guide in the Art Musuem and enjoys tending to her flower beds which are known for their beautiful bright colors.

Zachary Babblebrooks wants to be an architect when he grows up. He loves to build tree houses out of sticks and mud and oftentimes engages the help of his many friends. It is rare for Zachary to emerge from one of his building excursions not covered in clay and mud.

Misty Waters can often be found hanging around her father's Dental Office. She, too, wants to be a dentist when she grows up. She enjoys helping Father around the office by washing his dental instruments and filing his pantient's x-rays.
