Sylvanian Families Waters Beaver Family (Tomy)



UK Biography Information

Father Wade Waters is the best house builder and carpenter in the Sylvanian woods He can build you anything from a tree house to a large family home on the hills of Sylvania.

Mother Nancy Waters is a great gardener who grows the most beautiful flowers and delicious vegetables in the whole of Sylvania. She's famous for her miniature roses and sweet peas.

BrotherRoger Waters is very friendly and sporty, making him so popular he was voted head-boy of his school, a job he takes very seriously.

SisterMisty Waters day-dreams all the time. Her favourite day dream is of being a famous dancer; as the principle ballerina in Swan Lake of topping the bill in the theatre production of Cats.
