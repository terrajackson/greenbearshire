Sylvanian Families Sewing with Mother


From the box:

Sybil Clearwater is an expert needlewoman who spends many a happy hour teaching her daughter
Stephanie the art of sewing. Stephanie loves using her mother's sewing machine more than stitching her creations by hand, it's quicker and you don't prick your thumb! Sybil enjoys embroidery as she finds it very relaxing and it can be done whilst listening to the radio.
