UK Biography Information

Father Kenneth Furbanks loves acting and directing, telling people what to do with a big booming voice. He takes acting awfully seriously.

Mother Emma Furbanks calls everyone 'darling' or 'lovey'. That's because she's always so busy trying to remember her lines she forgets everyone's names!

Sister Greta Furbanks spends a lot of time on her own, so she can get on with the serious business of learning her lines.

Brother Douglas Furbanks who is known as Junior, spends most of his time in an imaginary world of his own - a world populated by pirates, white knights, and fire breathing dragons.
