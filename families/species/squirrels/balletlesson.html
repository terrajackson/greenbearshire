Sylvanian Families Ballet Lesson


From the box:

Jane Apple-Blossom teaches ballet. Her star student is her daughter Justina, who wants to practise all the time. Jane
loves watching her daughter dance so they move all the furniture to the sides of the room creating enough
space for Justina to dance freely while Jane accompanies her at the piano. Their favourite music is from The Nutcracker
and they love to swirl and spin around the room together.
