Sylvanian Families Walnut Squirrel Family



UK Biography Information

Father Cedric Walnut is never happier than when he is organising something. Whether it is a carnival, a wedding or a birthday party, Cedric will make sure it is a day to remember.

Mother Yardley Walnut is an expert beautician and hairdresser, Yardley loves nothing more than helping people to look their best. She always says: "Everyone has beauty in them and it's my job to help that shine through!'

Brother Ralph Walnut is definitely the outdoor type. His trusty bicycle has carried him on some of his most exciting woodland expeditions and he is very rarely seen without it.

Sister Saffron Walnut is just mad about the colour yellow and says her favourite things are sunshine and buttercups. Her wardrobe is full of yellow clothes and even her bedroom is painted in her favourite colour!
