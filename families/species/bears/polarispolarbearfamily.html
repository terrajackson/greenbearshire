UK Biography Information

Mother Barbara Polaris is a baker and makes delicious treats. She is well-known for her strawberry ice cream cakes and chocolate eclairs. When she isn't baking, she likes cooking delicious maels for the whole family. She has got a weakness for strawberry ice cream and loves to share it with little Beth in winter when it's cold outside an warm inside.

Father Peter Polaris is one of the best fishermen in town. He loves the sea and has spent a lot of his time studying it. He is always telling his family about his adventures on his boat and stories about faraway lands and oceans. He is easy to spot as he is always wearing his favourite scarf which Julia has knetted to keep him warm on his late night fishing expedtions.

Sister Julia Polaris is a gentle little girl who likes spending time at home knitting. She knits beautiful sweaters, gloves, and scarves for her friends and family which make them feel warm inside and out.

Baby Sister Beth Polaris is always eating, especially ice cream! She dreams of becoming an ice cream stand owner, just like Vincent and Violet Beauford. She likes snuggling in her bed with her favourite pink blanket that her big sister knitted for her and she treasures the vanilla white scarf her sister Julia knitted for her!
