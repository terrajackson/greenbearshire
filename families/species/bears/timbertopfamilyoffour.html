Sylvanian Families Timbertop Bear Family (USA, Tomy)


The Timbertops are one of the original Sylvanian Families and are a "big bear" mold family.
I love the Timbertops! This particular version is from the US, before the name was changed to Calico Critters.


Biography Information

Taylor Timbertop is a wonderful dad who hugs his 6 kids so much he's called "Daddy Hugs".

Rose Timbertop loves kids so much it's hard to tell which are hers. But Mama Rose knows!

Bud Timbertop likes all sports. Run, jump, swim...if it's a sport, always count him in!

Daisy Timbertop wants to be grown up. She helps Mama in the kitchen and gets to lick the spoon!
