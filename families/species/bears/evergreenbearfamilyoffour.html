Sylvanian Families Evergreen Grey Bear Family (USA, Tomy)


Like the Timbertops, the Evergreens are one of the original Sylvanian Families and are a "big bear"
mold family. I love the Originals! This particular version is from the US, before the name was
changed to Calico Critters.


Biography Information

Forrest Evergreen is the nicest doctor ever. Kids especially love the lemon drops he gives them.

Honeysuckle Evergreen loves reading to her kids at bedtime, naptime... anytime at all.

Preston Evergreen is the class clown. He's always being funny. The teacher even laughs sometimes.

Ashley Evergreen is a quick child. She thinks quickly, talks quickly, and moves quickly too.
