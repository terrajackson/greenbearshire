Calico Critters Biography Information

Boy twin Lucas loves playing and splashing around in nearby ponds. He likes floating in the pond and looking up at the beautiful blue sky, birds, and clouds. He splashes water on his sister all the time and they both go home wet from head to toe!

Girl twin Loreli constantly plays outside. She loves chasing butterflies and rolling around in the grass. She is always very cheerful and happy. When you see her you can't help but smile and feel all warm inside.
