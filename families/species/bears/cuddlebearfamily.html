Calico Critters Biography Information

Mother Carol is always smiling and is very loving. She likes to read and cook berry pies and chocolate chip cookies for her family.

Father Arthur is the mayor of Cloverleaf Corners. He is also the teacher at the Country Tree School, which is why he is always very busy. He likes to fishing on the weekends.

Brother Aidan loves to play sports, especially baseball and football. After a game or practice he loves to eat burgers at the Burger Cafe with his friends.

Sister Ava can be very shy at times. She is always helping her mother around the house. She likes to tend to the garden and grow beautiful flowers.
