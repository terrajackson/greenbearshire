Biography Information

Father Arthur Osborne is the teacher at Country Tree School.

Mother Carol Osborne likes to read, especially cook books! Her favourite things to bake are fruit pies and chocolate chip cookies.

Brother Aidan Osborne loves to play sports, especially football. But all that activity makes him very hungry and he can often be found tucking into a hamburger, his favourite food!

Sister Ava Osborne can be very shy at times and is happiest when outdoors tending the garden and admiring all the pretty flowers.
