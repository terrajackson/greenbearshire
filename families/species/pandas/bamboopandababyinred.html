Sylvanian Families Bamboo Panda Baby (Red)



UK Biography Information

Baby Brother Remi Bamboo is perhaps the only panda in the world who's not always black and white. That's because he loves to paint and is often covered in the stuff! Once (about six months ago) he painted himself, his bedroom and even his carpet bright pink. He had five weeks without pocket money before his parents forgave him! "When I'm a famous artist they'll think differently", he was last heard to say as he painted all his little sister's shoes pea green.
