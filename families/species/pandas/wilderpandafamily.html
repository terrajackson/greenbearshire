Calico Critters Wilder Panda Family



Calico Critters Biography Information

Mother Penelope is a nurse at Calico Critters Children's Hospital tending to broken bones and nasty colds. Her pockets are always full of lollipops to give to her injured or sick patients.

Father Percival owns his own bamboo fence business. In his free time he helps the children practice for their next big performance.

Brother Phillip's favorite thing to do is tap dance. He has won many competitions and looks forward to the Cloverleaf Corners dance-off held each year.

Sister Priscilla loves to perform in plays. She has a lovely singing voice too. When she's done with her homework, she will join her friends to create original plays which they perform for anyone who will watch.
