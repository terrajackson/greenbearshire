UK Biography Information

Baby Sister Lena Bamboo has arguably got the biggest smile you have ever seen. She's also got the most infectious giggle. Whenever she giggles (which is usually when she is meant to be quiet - like at Sylvanian weddings) everyone else starts to giggle too! That means she often gets her friends into trouble with serious grown ups who have forgotten how to giggle.
