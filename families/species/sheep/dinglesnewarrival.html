Sylvanian Families The Dingles' New Arrival


UK Biography Information

Dominic Dingle and his darling wife Rebecca have a new arrival in the form of a beautiful
baby girl they have called Katharina. This little bundle of joy has her very own newly decorated nursery and a brand new cot to sleep and play in. Katherarina has even learnt how to pull open
the drawers under her cot, where her mother stores all her toys and bedtime books. Both
Dominic and Rebecca are now very careful when lowering the drop side to her cot so that little
eyes don't see how it's done.
