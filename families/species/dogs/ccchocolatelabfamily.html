Calico Critters Chocolate Labrador Family



UK Biography Information

Mother Sienna makes delicous jams from the fruit in her farm. She sells her creations in the Supermarket for all the critters in Calico Village to enjoy.

Father Bruno is a very hard working farmer. He wakes up at 5 o'clock every morning. He grows all different kinds of fruits and vegetables, including corn, cabbage, apples, and grapes.

Sister Ruby likes spending time with her friends and having sleepovers at her house. She helps her mom cook veggie pizza and then they play games and talk all night.

Brother Charlie eats breakfast with his father every morning and helps him tend to the crops after school. He also likes to play tennis and roller skate around the park.
