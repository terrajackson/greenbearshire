Calico Critters Beagle Dog Family



Calico Critters Biography Information

Mother, Prunella is famous for the delicious desserts she makes. Her specialties include blueberry pie and cheesecake. On Sunday, she likes to make a big surprise cake and invite all children in CLoverleaf Corners to stop by the bakery and have a bite.

Father, Percy is the Master Baker in Cloverleaf Corners. He gets up very early in the morning to have fresh croissnts ready just in time for breakfast. In the evening, he often snoozes in front of the TV until his wife tells him it's time to go to bed.

Brother, Harold likes to run around and play with the boys. He is always quick to learn a new game and is set on winning every time. If he has to lose, he likes to put on a careless air and pretend as if nothing happened.

Sister, Antonia likes all things pretty. When someone orders a special cake for a birthday or wedding, she is the one to decorate it with icing, berries, and chocolate. She loves flowers and butterflies and her room is filled with all kinds of knick-knacks and souvenirs.
