Calico Critters Sunnybrook Spaniel Family


Calico Critters Biography Information

Mother, Grace is a very talented, famous actress. She often plays the lead role in plays and movies. Her children love watching her on TV! When she goes into town, every critter wants a picture with her and her autograph.

Father Marty is a famous chef and owns a restaurant in Cloverleaf Corners called "Sunnybrooks". His favorite dishes to cook are fettuccine alfredo, mozzarella sticks and pizza. His friends and family come to visit him all the time to taste his special meals. Marty has also written several cook books, which are all bes sellers.

Brother, Max is very intelligent and always gets A's in school. He likes to read a lot and learn about new things everyday. His favorite subject in school is Science. He recently made a volcano out of clay and won 1st place in the Science Fair!

Sister, Ginger is very energetic and likes to stay active. She is on her school's track team and soccer team. She's the fastest runner and always scores the most goals in her soccer games. She also likes to exercise in her spare time.
