Calico Critters Hopscotch Rabbit Family



The Hopscotch Rabbits are the Calico Critters (US) version of the Chocolate Rabbits,
with different names.


Calico Critters Biography Information

Mother, Heidi is always taking care of her family and keeps the house clean and nicely decorated. She likes cooking delicious meals and sewing clothes for her children. Her family thinks she is amazing and doesn't know how she gets so much done in such little time!

Father, Harlin enjoys planning all the fun community events in Calico Village. His family and friends always have a great time at any party he plans. On his days off, he likes to bake bread and take his family to the park for picnics.

Sister, Bell is vey cheerful and likes having fun with her family and friends. She often invites her friends to parties at her house and gives them delicious treats she bakes with her mother. She is also very good at drawing and has won lots of prizes in school competitions.

Brother, Skip is a great soccer player and is on his school's team. He can kick the ball further than any of his other teammates. He is always in a good mood and makes his friends laugh with his jokes. He tends to be very quiet in math class because he's not very good at it.
