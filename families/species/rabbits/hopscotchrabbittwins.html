Calico Critters Hopscotch Rabbit Twins



The Hopscotch Rabbits are the Calico Critters (US) version of the Chocolate Rabbits,
with different names.


Calico Critters Biography Information

Boy twin, Hopper Hopscotch could be a star for the "energizer bunny" commercial. He never tires of running around and can't remain still for a second. Sometimes even his energetic parents wish there were a battery inside their son that they could take out and enjoy a moment of rest.

Girl twin, Honey Hopscotch is her twin brother's total opposite. She loves to play quietly on the grass and can spend hours entertaining herself with flowers, birds, and butterflies.
