Sylvanian Families Beach Fun and Games


From the box:

The beach is calling and Auntie Bethany and daughter Sunny Blackberry can't wait to have some fun and are hoping that the sun keeps shining! There will be lots of things to keep everyone entertained from beach volley ball to a spot of surfing. They are both dressed ready for the beach in their pretty swimsuits. Sunny loves to wear her matching flip flops on the beach and mummy Bethany always makes sure that suncream is applied before heading outside.
