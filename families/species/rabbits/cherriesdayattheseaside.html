Cherrie Chocolate is Freya Chocolate's cousin!

UK Biography Information

All Sylvanians love to be beside the seaside and rabbit cousin Cherrie Chocolate is no exception! She has her special rubber ring and a very stylish lounger to sit on and relax in the sun, but if it gets too hot she can sit under her umbrella and have a refreshing glass of orange juice.
