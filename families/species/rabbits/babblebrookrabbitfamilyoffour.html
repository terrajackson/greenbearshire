Sylvanian Families Babblebrook Rabbit Family
(USA, Tomy)




The Babblebrooks are one of the original Sylvanian Families. This particular version is from
the US, when the name for the American line was still Sylvanian Families (rather than the current name, Calico Critters.) I'm especially fond of all the original families.

Biography Information

Rocky Babblebrook runs the General Store. If he doesn't sell it, you probably don't need it.

Crystal Babblebrook is the best cook in our whole woods. She even wrote a cookbook once!

Bubba Babblebrook is all ears. He can hear a bee buzz a block away. He says he hears grass grow.

Breezy Babblebrook likes to have her own way. Luckily, her way is a nice way.
