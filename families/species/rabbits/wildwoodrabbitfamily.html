Sylvanian Families Wildwood Rabbit Family




The Wildwoods are one of the original Sylvanian Families. This Tomy set is from the US.

Biography Information

Herb Wildwood loves to throw big parties. "If you've got friends, you've got everything."

Ginger Wildwood is known as the "hostess with the mostest". The "mostest" parties, that is.

Rusty Wildwood keeps the music going. He can even play the tambourine with his long ears.

Hollie Wildwood's favourite day is her birthday. And Christmas. And New Year's Eve...
