Calico Critters Hopscotch Rabbit Grandparents



The Hopscotch Rabbit Grandparents are the Calico Critters (US) version of the Chocolate Rabbit Grandparents, with different names. Glasses are included for both figures.


Calico Critters Biography Information

Grandfather, George is the captain of the Seaside Cruiser Houseboat. He loves traveling with his family and friends out on the open water.

Grandmother, Patricia likes to plan events and have fun parties on the Seaside Cruiser Houseboat. She also enjoys lounging on the sundeck, while drinking lemonade and chatting with her friends.
