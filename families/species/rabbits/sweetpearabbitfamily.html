Calico Critters Biography Information

Mother Nikki is a nurse in Calico Village and likes taking care of all the children and babies. She also likes decorating her home with lots of flowers and pictures of her family.

Father Ben is the doctor in Calico Village and helps all the critters stay in tip-top shape. He is always smiling and friendly to every critter he meets. He loves taking pictures of his family and making photo albums.

Sister Rebecca likes writing fairy tales, especially about princesses. She secretly would like to become a princess herself one day. She also loves to play dress up and dance at family parties.

Brother Oliver writes the most beautiful poems. All the girls at school like hearing his lovely poems during writing class. He also lieks to ice skate on the lake when it freezes in the winter.
