Baden & Mikaela's Camping Set



The classic vintage style of this set has made it my favorite out of all the camping sets.

From the box:

It's not often that brothers and sisters like doing the same thing, but Baden Renard (a Boy Scout) and Mikeala Renard (a Girl Guide) both love camping. They have everything they need for the great outdoors, tent, flagpole, open fire, and fireside stools, pots, pans, knives, and forks, plates, bottles of pop.
