Roxy Renard's Home Interior Set



This set is another of my favorites. Roxy came with sticker sheets to be used for paintings and to add trim to Sylvanian homes. The wheel on her sewing machine really turns.

From the box:

Roxy Renard is Sylvania's top interior designer. Roxy is always full of great decorating ideas anc comes complete with her large selection of friezes, sewing machine, stool, scissors, cotton reels, ruler, section of picture frames and pictures plus everything else she needs to brighten up a Sylvanian home.
