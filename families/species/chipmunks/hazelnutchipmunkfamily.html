Calico Critters Biography Information


Mother Savannah is a famous fashion stylist. Critter girls come from all over Cloverleaf Corners to have her pick out the best dress and accessories for their special occasion.

Father John is a furniture designer. All of the furniture he makes is very unique and beautiful. After work he loves cooking dinner for his family. His specialty is spaghetti.

Sister Sarah values her friends and family. She likes taking pictures of her favorite moments and keeping them in a photo album to remind her of all the great memories.

Brother Dominic is energetic and loves to climb trees around Cloverleaf Corners. He also likes to plant flowers and oak trees with his mother. He likes watching them grow in the spring.
