Sylvanian Families P.C. Bobby Roberts


P.C. Bobby Roberts is the only member of this family.

From the box:

P.C. "Bobby" Roberts always has a friend word for everyone he meets - except if they've been naughty! He rides about on his blue bike making sure everything in Sylvanian is just as it should be. Sometimes, he even gives children a ride in the little seat behind him. All in all, he's a jolly nice policeman.
