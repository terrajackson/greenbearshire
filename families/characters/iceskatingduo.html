Sylvanian Families Ice Skating Duo

From the box:
Christopher Tailbury and Jayne Golightly love ice skating and look forward to winter, a time when they can dust off their skates
and take to the ice. With a jump and a swizzle these two dazzle the crowds as they glide across the ice in a graceful blur. Their favourite routine with the crowds is the dance which ends dramatically with both of them stretched out flat on the ice.
