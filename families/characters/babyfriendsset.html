Calico Critters Baby Friends Set


From the box:

Hazelnut Chipmunk boy, Luke , loves to jump around all day. He especially likes to jump in the puddles after it rains. He also can't wait for his brother Dominic to teach him how to climb trees when he's a little older.

Hopscotch Rabbit girl, Grace , likes eating the chocolate croissants her father makes for her every day. When she greets other critters, she often has chocolate around her mouth. She also likes to feed and play with birds in the garden.

Silk Cat girl, Faith , loves all shiny things! She has a box full of treasures, such as pearls that her mother gave her and beautiful stones she found at the beach. On sunny days, she likes to watch the river sparkle for hours.
